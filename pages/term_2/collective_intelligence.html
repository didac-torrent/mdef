/collective_intelligence

(27_february_2022)

This brief course was an introduction to the Internet, the importance it has taken during the last 30 years, the infrastructure behind it and some new elements that are becoming popular nowadays, like cryptocurrencies and NFTs.

During the first session, Guillem and Victor made an introduction to the different layers internet has, separating the physical world from the digital one. We talked about protocols and the reasons why accessing the internet is free. We know World Wide Web but there are many other protocols that didn't become as popular but that can be widely used for other purposes (p.e. Gemini). The websites we visit every day are just platforms created following a specific protocol.

On the other hand, we also discussed which is the needed infrastructure for us to use the internet in our daily lives.
